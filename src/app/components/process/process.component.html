Investment Casting Process

Investment casting is a complex process where a molten metal is poured into a ceramic shell to create small, precise metal components.
Its original name "lost wax casting," refers to the original wax pattern the ceramics are formed around, then melted and
replaced with alloys of stainless steel, aluminum, cobalt, carbon steel, brass, bronze, and other metals.
