Our Story

Adaptix Valves and Fittings Ltd is firmly established as one of the leading supplier of Industrial Valves, Butt weld fittings, Investment Casting ( Lost Wax Process ) Fittings, Forged fittings, Flanges . The materials we supply are all of a high quality and are in accordance with ASTM, DIN, EN and ISO standards.

We supply complete packages of Industrial Valves, Butt weld fittings, Investment Casting ( Lost Wax Process ) Fittings, Forged fittings, Flanges in many different steel grades such as Carbon steel, Stainless steel, Alloy steel, Nickel Alloy, Duplex and Super Duplex steel. As well as offering standard products we also offer special pieces according to customer drawing whatever the requirement is for fittings or flanges.

The principles and standards of Adaptix Valves and Fittings Ltd has made the company one of the most reputed trading organizations and we were able to satisfy all of our Regional and Global customers.

We offer professional management of small and large scale projects, short lead times, on-time delivery and most importantly high quality products. Our materials are used in critical applications, such as Chemical and Petrochemical plants, Oil installations, Power plants, Shipyards and Offshore platforms.
